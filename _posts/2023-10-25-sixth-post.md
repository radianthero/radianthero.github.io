Why flat colors? Twitch emotes biggest file is 112x112. For comparison, the Atari 2600 has a resolution of 160x192, the NES has 256 x 240. There's very little room to make things in, so I think flat colors help make things pop out a lot more. Also I tried to not have things that were related to me/the stream, instead of trying to make it more "generic" like a persona.
