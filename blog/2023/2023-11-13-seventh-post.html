Speaking of Something Interesting

I came back from Battle at the Border, a Smash 64 tourney in Arizona. I won't discuss much in this post as I want to create a whole dedicated post to it, to partner with a vlog that I have planned. Lots of pictures were taken during the traveling, and I'm excited to talk about them. This was the first event I went to out of state for, basically one part of the states to another. But I loved my time. That's not to say that I didn't do any drawing before the event.

What Is This Man Cooking

I made an emote for a friend, which is actually a remake of an older one. The old one was probably one of my least favorite ones, so I was happy when he wanted me to work on it again! Overall, I'm happy with how this turned out. Sure, it's another shorter one, but a picture is a picture /s.

It's of the character Ciel from Tsukihime/Melty Blood eating a fried curry, something she's fond of. There are plans for more drawings, but I want to work on the vlog, which may take a while. Unsure yet... it is art, just in a different format. Until next time!
